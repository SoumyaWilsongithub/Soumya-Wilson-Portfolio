Kisan_Sathi

The Challenge: Farmers in rural areas often suffer from information asymmetry, leading to suboptimal sales prices for their crops due to a lack of real-time market data.

The Implementation: I engineered a robust Spring Boot and MySQL backend to handle live agricultural datasets, ensuring high performance and reliable data delivery. I integrated MPAndroidChart for historical trend visualization and a Firebase notification system to alert users of instant price drops.

The Design: I built a clean, accessible XML interface in Android Studio specifically tailored for rural accessibility, significantly reducing navigation complexity for non-technical users.
